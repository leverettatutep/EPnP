All the points are columns.
Row 4 is the last row of vectors and points.
Last row of all points are 1.
Last row of all points are 0.
Transforms are 4 by 4 matrices.
Element 4,4 is 1.
Scaled transforms are done in the following way:
First displace based on column 4.
Then spin the frame based on the upper left 3 by 3.
Multiply the x,y,z by scale.
